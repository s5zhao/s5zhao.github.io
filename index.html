About关于我

I am Shengwu Zhao. I received my Ph.D. from the School of Automation at Beijing Institute of Technology in June 2026, majoring in Control Science and Engineering, with a focus on Navigation, Guidance and Control. I received my bachelor's degree from the School of Xuteli, Beijing Institute of Technology, in July 2020.

我是赵生武，2026年6月博士毕业于北京理工大学自动化学院，专业为控制科学与工程，导航制导与控制。2020年7月本科毕业于北京理工大学徐特立学院。

My research interests include underwater geophysical field matching navigation, inertial navigation, and multi-source fusion navigation. I am dedicated to long-duration high-precision underwater positioning.

我的研究方向为水下地球物理场匹配导航、惯性导航、多源融合导航等，致力于研究水下长航时高精度定位。
